About Me

The Person Behind Her Finance Journey

Hi, you don't know me, so let me introduce myself. I am Jemimah, a 20-year-old biology student at Durham Uni, who loves sport, cosy evenings with friends, and (I hate to say it) but an overpriced iced latte. While figuring out my own path and exploring finance, I realised how rare it was to find a space that felt genuinely supportive for women starting out, particularly in such a male-dominated field where women have a plethora of struggles and challenges everyday.

Why I Started
I wanted to create the kind of community I would have loved to discover at the start of my own journey.

This platform is meant to feel encouraging and practical, with room for ambition, uncertainty, experience, and growth all at once. While procrastinating revising for my Finals, I kept coming back to the same thought: there should be a place where women can talk honestly about finance, careers, confidence, and the lessons they had to learn the hard way. I wanted to build something that felt positive, useful, and real.
