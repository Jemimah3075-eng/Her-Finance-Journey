About Her Finance Journey

Inspiring, Empowering And Supporting Women In Finance.

Her Finance Journey is designed to make finance a more welcoming, inclusive and a less intimidating environment where women can thrive. The aim is to offer a mix of encouragement, practical guidance, and community.

Message from the Founder: I will be sharing reflections and important lessons I have learnt and am conintinuing to learn on my own finance journey, and I hope to create a space where women can share theirs, ask questions, and support each other.

Our Mission

Our Three Goals

Our mission is to create a community of women backing women to suceed in their finance careers. We aim to create a thoughtful, encouraging environment that supports and inspires women to take the next step with confidence.

Provide helpful, practical advice I wish I had known earlier.
Create a strong, safe, supportive environment for women.
Help women grow (personally and professionally) and strive for success.
