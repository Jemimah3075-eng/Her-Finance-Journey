Join A Community Of Women In Finance At All Stages Of Their Careers

Her Finance Journey is an encouraging space for women who want to feel more connected in this competitive industry, whilst learning from the mistakes I have made and am continuing to make in my journey. Our aim is to make women feel informed, confident, and supported as they navigate their career in Finance. Whether you are just thinking about starting a career in Finance, growing your career, or a veteran of the industry, this community is here to support and inspire you to step out of your comfort zone.
